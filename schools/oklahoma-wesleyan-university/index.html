"An OKWU education prioritizes the transformation of the whole person for the whole world. We help students discover, live, and deepen their relationship in Jesus Christ to influence culture with Grace & Truth." https://www.okwu.edu/about/

Influencing culture with the Grace & Truth of Jesus Christ. https://www.okcu.edu/campus/home

The mission of Oklahoma Wesleyan University is to live out what it means to be a Christ-centered university, modeling a way of thought, a way of life, and a way of faith. It is a place of serious study, honest questions, and critical engagement, all in the context of a liberal arts community that honors the Primacy of Jesus Christ, the Priority of Scripture, the Pursuit of Truth, and the Practice of Wisdom.

Trevecca Nazarene University

Church of the Nazarene

We believe that faith should be at the center of all things. That’s why, as a university, we come together multiple times a week to worship, learn, praise and grow in chapel. This time allows our students, staff and faculty to come together in fellowship to deepen their relationship with Christ and each other. Our students are also encouraged to serve—whether that be through one of our annual service projects, various student-led ministries or within chapel. https://www.trevecca.edu/about

Founded in 1901, Trevecca is committed to holistic education, encouraging students to grow intellectually, socially, emotionally, physically and spiritually. Located approximately one mile from thriving downtown Nashville, Trevecca provides a liberal arts education while guiding students to find God’s calling on their lives through traditional undergraduate programs, continuing and graduate studies and online offerings. https://www.trevecca.edu/

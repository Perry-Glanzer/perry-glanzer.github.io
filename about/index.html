    • Perry Glanzer

    Perry L. Glanzer (Ph.D., Univeristy of Southern California) is an esteemed professor specializing in Educational Foundations at Baylor University. Moreover, he serves as a Resident Scholar at the prestigious Baylor Institute for Studies of Religion.

    Throughout his career, Glanzer has authored or co-authored over one hundred journal articles and book chapters. His research endeavors encompass diverse, including moral education, faith-based higher education, and the intricate relationship between religion and education.

    Glanzer holds the position of Editor-in-Chief for the publication, Christian Scholar’s Review, a prominent academic journal, and curates the Christ Animating Learning blog. His passion and expertise revolve around the realm of moral education and the profound connections between Christianity and the field of education.
